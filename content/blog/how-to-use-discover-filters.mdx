The **Discover** pages are the fastest way to go from "I don't know what to watch" to "found it." Here's how to get the most out of them.

## 1. Start broad, then narrow

Head to [Discover Movies](/movies/discover) or [Discover TV Shows](/tv/discover) and pick a genre first. Combining two or three genres (like *Action* + *Comedy*) often surfaces hidden gems that a single-genre search would miss.

## 2. Sort by what matters to you

- **Most Popular** — what everyone's watching right now
- **Highest Rated** — critically and audience acclaimed
- **Newest First** — fresh releases
- **Oldest First** — dig into classics

## 3. Use the year filter to time-travel

Want to relive a specific year in film? Set the year filter and pair it with "Highest Rated" to get that year's best-reviewed titles instantly.

## 4. Bookmark your filtered views

Every filter combination is reflected in the URL, so you can bookmark or share a specific discovery view — like "Highest rated Sci-Fi movies from 2020" — with friends.

Happy discovering!
