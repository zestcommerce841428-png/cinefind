Finding the right movie to watch shouldn't take longer than actually watching it. That's why we built **CineFind** — a fast, modern search and discovery experience for movies, TV shows, and the people who make them.

## What you can do with CineFind

- **Search everything at once.** Movies, TV shows, and people, all from a single search box.
- **Discover with real filters.** Genre, release year, rating, and sort order — no more endless scrolling.
- **Go deep on every title.** Cast, crew, trailers, reviews, similar titles, and where to watch — all on one page.
- **Track what you love.** Sign in with your TMDB account to save favorites, build a watchlist, and rate what you've seen.

## Powered by TMDB

CineFind is built on top of [The Movie Database (TMDB)](https://www.themoviedb.org/), giving you access to one of the largest, most actively maintained movie and TV datasets on the web — updated constantly by a global community of contributors.

We're just getting started. More features, more content, and more ways to discover your next favorite movie are on the way.
